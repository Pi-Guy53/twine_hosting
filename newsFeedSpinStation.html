Spin Stations and you

Spin Stations, your marvelous and wonderful deep space destination.

2/2/2084
Robert Robertson
Spin Stations, so called for their centrifuge based gravity, are a common sight orbiting planets, moons and large asteroids, and are home to many spacefarers.

Spin Stations, often confused with High Ports, the primary difference being High ports are located on a planetoid/asteroid, and Spin Stations being in orbit.

There are many things to do aboard Spin Stations, as they are designed to be a permanent home to Spacefarers and their families. They contain schools, shopping centers, recreational areas, sports fields, and even vast hydroponic farms.
In addition, Spin Stations house markets, economic facilities, and are thus the economic centers for their sector of space.

While Spin Stations are always inside of either UNE, JSC, or PNPC space, they tend to operate under their own jurisdiction. Those on the outer edges of space are allowed significantly more freedoms in their operations, and while some of these Spin Stations are described as lawless, the majority keep their own order. Either with Private Military Contractors (PMCs), or Private Enforcers (PE).
Only in the cases of massive unrest, or prevailing problems do the JSC or PNPC need to intervene, while the UNE is constantly micromanaging their own Spin Stations.

In short, Spin Stations keep their own little worlds spinning.
